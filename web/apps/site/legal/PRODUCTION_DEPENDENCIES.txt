ppoker site production dependency notices
=========================================

Notice scope
------------

This notice covers the direct third-party JavaScript dependencies declared by
web/apps/site/package.json, relevant browser runtime packages brought in by
React, Motion, and ONNX Runtime, and the handwriting model/runtime additions.
Versions are pinned by web/pnpm-lock.yaml. License texts and upstream notices
for the packages listed within this scope are distributed beside this file.

This is not a complete dependency or license audit of the ppoker repository or
the complete built site. In particular, the pre-existing @ppoker/web-client,
ppoker-core, and Rust crates compiled into the web client's WebAssembly are not
enumerated or re-audited here. They remain governed by the repository's existing
dependency and licensing process.
